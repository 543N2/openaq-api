Colombia Air Quality Data visualizer

Description:


Colombia Air Quality Data Visualizer is a tool for obtaining and plotting real Air Quality data from the OpenAQ API, that builds a RAW and a SMOOTH data chart for the selected parameters.


The RAW chart shows a maximum of 24 datapoints per day. The SMOOTH chart shows the processed data based on the Air Quality Index stablished in the Resolution 2254 of 2017 and in the Protocol for the Monitoring of Air Quality, by the current Ministry of the Environment and Sustainable Development of Colombia.


How to use:


  1. Choose the from and to dates to query.
  2. Choose location to activate the available monitoring stations.
  3. Choose station to activate the available pollutants.
  4. Choose pollutant to complete the query.
  5. Click the Plot button to generate the charts.

Suggestions:


  • Do not forget to fill all the information before clicking Plot button.
  • Make sure the to date is more recent than the fromdate.
  • Feel free to update the parameters and plot a new chart.
  • The charts may have blanks. These are anoumalous datapoints filtered from the source.

Troubleshooting:


  • If an error pops up, please verify the dates and try again.
  • Sometimes the raw data chart is plotted even after the error pops up. It happens if there is not enough valid data for smooth calculations.
  • If something unexpected happens, please press F5 on your keyboard to refresh the page.
